

- Turn based gameplay.
    - Initiative will be used to determine turn order.
    - Players may invest resources to acquire bonuses to initiative.
- Hexagonal grid based map.
- Unit based combat.
- Terrain.
	- Affects movement of units.
	- Affects the resource generation of districts placed on top.
* Resource management.
-  Placeable districts.
    - Generates resources dependent on the terrain of the tile.
    - Generates resources dependent on adjacent districts.
* Military and district perk-tree.
